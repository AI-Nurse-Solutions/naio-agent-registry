## What this is

The NAIO Agent Registry is a curated, governed library of open-source AI agents, selected and contextualized for **nurses and healthcare entrepreneurs**. Unlike a raw "awesome list," every entry carries a stewardship layer:

- **EDENA tier** (🟢 Green / 🟡 Yellow / 🟠 Orange / 🔴 Red) — how much governance the agent demands
- **Autonomy level** (Hermes L0–L7) — how independently it acts
- **Claim vs. Proof** — what the project says it does vs. what is actually demonstrated
- **Nurse-entrepreneur use case** — what *you* could build with it
- **PHI / safety boundary** — explicit warnings where patient data or clinical risk is involved
- **Verdict** — Adopt / Pilot / Watch / Avoid

## ⚠️ Important: what inclusion does and does not mean

Listing in this registry is **NOT**:
- a clinical validation or endorsement for patient care
- a certification, accreditation, or NAIO seal of safety
- a guarantee of fitness for any healthcare purpose
- legal, medical, or compliance advice

Listing **IS**: a nurse-stewarded, transparent assessment of an open-source tool's design, maturity, and governance profile, to help nurses learn and build responsibly. **You and your organization remain responsible for any clinical, legal, or safety decision.** Never enter PHI into any tool without your organization's governance approval.
